Our Story

Our Story

The journey that
inspired Dralwa

Dralwa Journeys was born from a deeply personal journey.

A serious illness forced me to pause at a time when I was rushing through life. In that stillness, I began to question what it truly means to live, to be present, and to connect. As I healed, I started travelling differently. I spent less time chasing destinations and more time immersing myself in the people, places, and moments that make life meaningful.

Through these experiences, I discovered that connection is at the heart of everything — connection to place, to community, to nature, and ultimately to ourselves. Dralwa Journeys is an extension of that realisation. It is an invitation to engage deeply with places as they naturally unfold, and to travel in a way that fosters genuine connection.

What began as a personal journey of healing has become an offering to travellers seeking a deeper, more meaningful way to experience the world.
