Our Story

Born from Wandering,
Built on Purpose

Dralwa Journeys began as a personal journey — one that turned into a calling.

Founded in the heart of Thimphu, we are a team of Bhutanese travel designers, mountain guides, and wellness practitioners who believe that the most meaningful journeys are the ones that change you from the inside out.

Bhutan is unlike anywhere else on earth. It is a kingdom that has chosen wellbeing over wealth, forests over factories, and culture over conformity. We exist to share that vision — thoughtfully, authentically, and with deep respect for this sacred land and its people.

Every itinerary we design is a conversation, a collaboration, and ultimately a gift — to you and to Bhutan.

What Guides Us

The Principles Behind Every Journey

01

Intentional Travel

We believe every journey should have a purpose beyond sightseeing. We design experiences that invite reflection, connection, and genuine transformation.

02

Authentic Immersion

We go beyond the tourist trail — into monasteries, farmhouses, forest paths and festivals that reveal the true spirit of Bhutan and its people.

03

Responsible Stewardship

Bhutan's magic depends on its preservation. We operate with a low-impact, high-value philosophy — supporting local communities and protecting natural heritage.

04

Personalised Care

No two travellers are the same. We listen deeply before we design, tailoring every detail to align with your pace, interests, and inner compass.

05

Holistic Wellbeing

From forest bathing in ancient valleys to traditional Sowa Rigpa healing, we weave wellness into the fabric of every itinerary.

06

Lasting Connections

Our relationship with you doesn't end at the airport. Many of our guests become lifelong friends — and return to Bhutan year after year.
